Markaz imam muqbil

The Markaz Imam Muqbil Center in Kaduna, Nigeria was established With The aim of propagating the true teachings of Islam according to the understanding of the rightly guided and pious predecessors with particular focus on Tauhid, Aqeedah, Manhaj Quran, Fiqh, Hadith, Sunnah, Arabic etc with an emphasis on teaching their true meanings under the guidance of Sheikh Abu Aneesah Ya'qub Ibrahim. (May Allah have mercy on him and grant him success in adhering to the Sunnah.) Students at this center study various branches of Islamic jurisprudence, focusing on the importance of Tawheed (oneness of Allah) alongside their other studies like Aqeedah, Manhaj Quran, Fiqh, Hadith and Sunnah, language
